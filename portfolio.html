Investment Strategies

Tailored approaches for different investor profiles

Conservative Strategy

Our conservative strategy focuses on capital preservation and stable income generation. This approach is designed for investors with a shorter time horizon or lower risk tolerance.

50% Bonds
30% Cash
20% Stocks
  • Focus on high-quality bonds and dividend stocks
  • Emphasis on income generation
  • Lower volatility
  • Capital preservation

Balanced Strategy

Our balanced strategy seeks to provide a mix of growth and income with moderate risk. This approach is suitable for investors with a medium-term time horizon.

40% Stocks
40% Bonds
15% Alternatives
5% Cash
  • Diversified exposure across asset classes
  • Balance between growth and income
  • Moderate volatility
  • Regular rebalancing

Growth Strategy

Our growth strategy aims to achieve long-term capital appreciation with a higher allocation to equities. This approach is designed for investors with a longer time horizon who can tolerate higher volatility.

70% Stocks
20% Bonds
10% Alternatives
  • Focus on growth-oriented equities
  • Global diversification
  • Higher expected returns
  • Tactical adjustments based on market conditions

Aggressive Strategy

Our aggressive strategy seeks maximum capital appreciation with a high allocation to equities and alternative investments. This approach is suitable for investors with a long time horizon and high risk tolerance.

85% Stocks
15% Alternatives
  • High exposure to growth sectors
  • Emerging market opportunities
  • Maximum growth potential
  • Strategic positioning for long-term appreciation
