Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer service line for immediate assistance.

Can I schedule a virtual consultation?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video conference. You can schedule a virtual meeting through our online booking system or by contacting our office directly.

Do I need to make an appointment to visit your office?

While we welcome walk-ins, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that a specialist in your area of interest is available to assist you. This helps us provide you with the most efficient and personalized service.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can share your experience by completing our customer satisfaction survey, writing a review on our social media pages, or contacting our customer experience team directly at feedback@panurgic.com.
